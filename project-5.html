Description

Idea

The thought started randomly, inspired by Rajasthan Tourism campaigns. When we think of animals in Jaipur, familiar images come to mind camels, elephants, horses. But rarely does anyone think of a deer. That contrast intrigued me. Deer feels unexpected, almost fancy, and that’s exactly why it became the centre of the idea to show a side of Jaipur people don’t usually anticipate.

Execution

We built a simple storyline around a tourist exploring Jaipur, clicking pictures of animals as one would expect. As he casually walks through the city, he suddenly spots a deer moving through the streets surprising, confusing, and completely unexpected. He instinctively clicks a photo, capturing the moment. The film ends on a simple thought: in Jaipur, it’s not always what you expect to see and that’s the beauty of it.
